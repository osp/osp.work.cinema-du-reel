Cinéma du réel
==============

Poster, program, installation
-----------------------------

A cartography of the selection of the documentary film festival Cinéma du Réel, 
executed as poster/program and as a full scale map installation on the floors of
the Centre Pompidou where the festival takes place.

Commisioned by BPI, Bibliothèque Publique d'information de Paris

Design: Harrison – Ludi
